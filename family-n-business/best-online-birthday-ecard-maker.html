Top 10 Best Birthday eCard Online Makers [2020]

Ollie Mattison

Feb 21,2020• Proven solutions

0

We all lead fast-paced lives which often prevents us from being with those we care about the most on days that are special to them. Just because you can’t be with your friends and family on their birthdays, doesn’t mean that you don’t care about them. eCards offer a quick and simple way to show those who are closest to you that you are thinking about them on their birthday.

Creating and sending a birthday eCard is a straightforward process that will take only a few moments of your time, and it will show your loved ones how much they mean to you. There are hundreds of eCard makers online, which makes finding the one you can rely on a bit complicated. That’s why we did the research for you and selected the ten best birthday eCard online makers you can use to send best wishes to your friends and family on their birthdays.

10 Best Birthday eCard Online Makers [Free & Paid]

You don’t need graphic design skills in order to use any of the birthday eCard online makers we included in this article since each of them offers a huge selection of customizable templates. You just have to come up with a unique birthday message and choose the visual style of the eCard, and the platform you selected will do the rest for you. Here are the ten best birthday eCard online makers.

1. Ojolie

Price: Free, pricing plans start at $14.00 for a one-year subscription

This online eCard maker offers animated eCards for almost any occasion, which means that you can use it to wish someone a Merry Christmas or a happy birthday. The platform’s birthday category contains hundreds of easily customizable animated eCards that all have names that match the mood they convey. Love Tweetings, Party Animals or Birthday Dance are just a few among countless options you are going to have if you choose to create birthday eCards with Ojolie.

However, you must create an account on this platform before you can start customizing and sending animated birthday eCards to your friends and family. Ojolie also offers the option to send eCards to Facebook or save them to your computer. In case you opt to use the free version of the platform you will be able to send only free eCards.

Pros

  • Offers hundreds of differently themed birthday eCards
  • Quick preview of all eCards
  • Inserting text is a straightforward process
  • Excellent social sharing capabilities

Cons

  • Ojolie doesn’t offer monthly subscription models
  • You can only send free eCards with the free version of the platform

2. Blue Mountain

Price: Free, pricing plans start at $4.99 per month

You can create a personalized birthday eCard with Blue Mountain in just a few clicks, as you just have to select a picture or a video and then add the text. The birthday eCards this platform offers are divided into different categories such as Family, Kids or Friends, which helps you speed up the search for the perfect eCard.

You can only preview eCards as an unregistered user, and you must create an account on Blue Mountain if you want to create or send eCards via email or Facebook. In addition to hundreds of birthday eCards, Blue Mountain also offers a rich selection of printable cards, postcards and gift cards you can use to wish a happy birthday to those that matter to you the most.

Pros

  • Blue Mountain offers hundreds of birthday eCards
  • It is easy to personalize a birthday eCard
  • Neatly organized website
  • Birthday stationaries are also available

Cons

  • Sign-in is mandatory
  • Sending options could be better

3. Greetings Island

Price: Free

Making different types of invitations, eCards or calendars with Greeting Island platform won’t cost you a single cent. Furthermore, you don’t need an account on this platform in order to personalize an eCard. All birthday eCards are organized in Kids, For Her and For Him categories, and you can also upload your own images if you don’t want to use any of the templates this platform offers. The vast majority of birthday eCards on this platform are also available as printable cards so that you can choose if you want to send them over the Internet or deliver them in person.

Moreover, Greetings Island lets you choose from a broad range of fonts, and it allows you to adjust the size and color of the text. The platform also features a rich selection of stickers that can be added to birthday eCards with a single click.

Pros

  • Free and easy to use
  • Powerful birthday eCard customization tools
  • Enables users to upload their own images
  • Features hundreds of stickers

Cons

  • The platform displays ads
  • You can only send birthday eCards via email or Facebook

4. Adobe Spark

Price: Free, pricing plans start at $12.89 per month

Adobe Spark is much more than just a simple online birthday eCard maker since Adobe created this app with the aim of helping digital marketers produce visual content effortlessly. This web-based platform offers hundreds of templates that enable you to create personalized birthday eCards.

The process of making a birthday eCard with Adobe Spark is simple since you just have to select dimensions, opt for one of the available themes, insert images and text and then decide if you want to post the birthday eCard on social media, save it to your computer or print it. Keep in mind that all eCards you create with the free version of Adobe Spark are going to be watermarked. What’s more, you need to have at least basic design skills if you want to use this platform to make birthday eCards.

Pros

  • Adobe Spark lets you choose the shape and size of your birthday eCards
  • All themes are fully customizable
  • You can save all your creations
  • Printing birthday eCards is easy

Cons

  • You must upgrade to one of the available subscription options if you want to have access to the full library of Adobe’s fonts
  • All birthday eCards created with the free version of Adobe Spark are going to be watermarked

5. Canva

Price: Free, pricing plans start at $9.95 per month

This platform is aimed at graphic designers, which means that creating a birthday eCard with Canva is a routine task. There are hundreds of birthday templates to choose from, but you must first sign in to your account before you can access any of them. The platform lets you upload your own photos, change backgrounds and adjust the size of textboxes.

Optionally, you can choose preset font combinations or pick a font from the platform library of fonts. Once you’re done designing a birthday eCard, you can choose if you want to print it, post it on social media or send it via email. Canva also enables you to get a shareable link you can send via messaging apps so that the recipients can download the eCard effortlessly.

Pros

  • Offers professional graphic design tools
  • Excellent collaboration features
  • Equipped with hundreds of customizable birthday eCard templates
  • Versatile file sharing options

Cons

  • Requires basic graphic design skills
  • The free version of Canva offers only limited options

6. FotoJet

Price: Free, pricing plans start at $6.99 per month

Even though FotoJet is primarily an online photo editor, the platform also provides a host of graphic design features. The extensive template collection is organized into different categories, so you just have to pick the Birthday category from the drop-down menu and choose one of the available options. Editing the textboxes in each of the templates is easy, as you just have to click on the textbox, select the font, adjust the size of the text and choose its color. FotoJet also offers a few text effects, which enables you to add an outline or glow to each letter you type.

Even though you don’t have to sign in to your FotoJet account, you won’t be able to download or share your designs before you register and log in to your account on this platform. Furthermore, purchasing the Plus version of FotoJet will grant you access to more than 800 premium templates.

Pros

  • FotoJet features a huge library of royalty-free photos
  • Each birthday eCard template is easily customizable
  • Features templates that can be used for a broad range of purposes
  • Adding clipart to eCards requires a minimum amount of effort

Cons

  • The free version of FotoJet offers a limited number of eCard templates
  • Designing an eCard with FotoJet can be too technical for inexperienced graphic designers

7. Smilebox

Price: Free, pricing plans start at $5.00 per month

Designing a slideshow, a flyer or an eCard with Smilebox requires you to go through four easy steps. After you choose the eCard design, you just have to upload the images, add the text and share your creation on social media or via email. Besides birthday eCards that contain photos, Smilebox also offers templates that feature birthday videos.

So, in addition to customizing the materials, you include in the video you can also create soundtracks for all birthday eCards you create with this platform. Even though anyone can create an account on Smilebox for free, purchasing a subscription plan is necessary if you want to remove the watermark from your creations or have the option to upload your own music. What’s more, you are not going to be able to send birthday eCards via email, if you decide to use the free version of Smilebox.

Pros

  • Grants users complete control over the design of eCards
  • Offers video birthday eCards
  • Features a music library
  • Affordable

Cons

  • Limited file sharing options
  • Register required
  • Importing your own music is only possible on the paid version of Smilebox

8. Happy Birthday to You

Price: Free

If you are looking for a way to create a funny birthday eCard, the Happy Birthday to You platform may have a perfect solution. You can choose from a number of animated singing birthday eCards and even select the music style. All you need to do to select a Blues, Rap or Country music genre is click on it and the platform will automatically play the song.

Even so, the outdated webpage design, as well as poor quality of animations, make this birthday eCard maker an unreliable choice. Happy Birthday to You uses the Adobe Flash Player, and you must make sure that Flash is enabled in your web browser. Besides creating singing birthday eCards, you can also use this platform to make singing anniversary eCards, but you should keep in mind that the process of sending these eCards is complicated.

Pros

  • Offers an easy way to create funny birthday eCards
  • Lets users choose from a variety of music genres
  • Features hundreds of animations
  • All animations can be used for free

Cons

  • No social sharing options
  • The platform uses Flash

9. JibJab

Price: Free, pricing options start at $2.00

JibJab features hundreds of birthday eCard video templates that allow you to turn your friends into rock stars, cartoon or movie characters. The platform’s face recognition technology analyzes the images you upload and adds the faces to the templates videos. Moreover, each template lets you add a different number of faces, so you can choose if you want to create a card with just one or up to five faces. The platform saves all the faces you upload which enables you to use them as many times as you want.

Once the video is customized you just have to type in the birthday message and choose if you want to share the video on social media or send it through email. You won’t be able to share the birthday eCards you create with JibJab before you sign in to your account.

Pros

  • JibJab features hundreds of birthday eCard video templates
  • Equipped with a powerful face recognition technology
  • Creating a birthday eCard with JibJab takes just a couple of minutes
  • Offers an inexpensive way to have fun with your friends

Cons

  • Birthday eCard process is automatic
  • No photo-based templates

10. Hallmark eCards

Price: Free, pricing options start at $5.00 per month

Hallmark is the oldest manufacturer of greeting cards in the United States, so it is hardly a surprise that their birthday eCards are also among the best you can find on the Internet. Their collection of birthday eCard video templates contains hundreds of different options, which enables you to choose the one that is best suited for the person to which you want to send it to.

Personalizing each of the Hallmark’s eCards is easy, as you just have to decide if you want to use a handwritten font and choose the color of the background and the text. Afterward, you can proceed to write the birthday message and click on the Address and Send button. However, you must be a registered user of the Hallmark eCards platform in order to send birthday eCards to your friends or family members.

Pros

  • Offers eCards for different types of occasions
  • Elegantly designed birthday eCard video templates
  • Equipped with handwritten fonts
  • Cheap monthly subscription

Cons

  • None whatsoever, Hallmark is a perfect birthday eCard platform

Conclusion

Each of the birthday eCard makers we featured in this article enables you to send the best wishes to your loved ones on their birthday even if you are not with them. However, eCards can sometimes get stuck in spam folders, which is the reason why it is better to save your creations to your computer before sending them to someone’s email address.

Besides sending eCards, you can also make a birthday slideshow video with photos and music for the loved one. Creating such birthday slideshow is easy with Filmora9, you can download the trial version to test yourself.
