How to Make an Advertisement Video easily

Below are the steps to let you know how to make an advertisement video. You can follow these steps to make an amazing video ad. Take a look!

Step 1: Import video clips

After you download Filmora9 Business, open it, and click New Project. Click import media files here to import multiple videos at one time. You can drag it onto the timeline.

Step 2: Basic editing: cutting/trimming

You can drag the red playhead to where you want to cut video first and then click the scissor icon in the toolbar. Drag it to another place to cut the clip again. Now, you can get the trimmed clips. Here you can also right-click on the clip and choose color correction to make the color better. You can also add various transitions to make the videos smooth.

Step 3: Add the filters/text/audio

Now you can add a filter to make a cinematic effect. Go to the Effects at the top and choose filters. Just choose one filter you like. You can also go to tiles to add text effects to highlight your key message. If you want to make your video ad more engaging, why not use audio? Go to the Audio tab to choose the different audio styles. All of them are free to use. You can also explore other amazing features.


Step 4: Export video ad


Now all of the basic editings are done. Click Export. A pop-up windows will appear. You can choose different video formats, including MP4, MOV, AVI, and more. You can also directly upload the video to YouTube or Vimeo. Click OK to start exporting.
